# Onboarding Feature

## Purpose
Owns first-run onboarding and module introduction flows.

## Responsibilities
- Present onboarding pages and widgets.
- Coordinate onboarding providers and future onboarding use cases.

## What Belongs Here
- Onboarding pages, widgets, providers, models, and use cases.

## What Should NOT Be Placed Here
- Auth, dashboard, loan, or payment business logic.
