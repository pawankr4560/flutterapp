# Agriculture & Pestiside Feature

## Purpose
Manages field health records, spray applications, and pesticide/fertilizer stock for agriculture businesses.

## Responsibilities
- Show field records with health status filters.
- Log pesticide or fertilizer spray applications.
- Track available pesticide and fertilizer stock.

## What Belongs Here
- Agriculture-specific entities, providers, pages, and widgets.
- Field, crop, spray, and stock logic.

## What Should NOT Be Placed Here
- Dashboard layout logic.
- Shared UI widgets used across multiple features.
- Authentication, payments, or unrelated business modules.
