# Core

## Purpose
Holds cross-cutting infrastructure that is reusable across the whole FinHub app.

## Responsibilities
- Design-system theme tokens and app-wide widgets.
- Shared constants, platform services, utilities, and extensions.

## What Belongs Here
- `constants/`, `theme/`, `widgets/`, `services/`, `utils/`, and `extensions/`.

## What Should NOT Be Placed Here
- Feature-specific screens, models, repositories, or business rules.
